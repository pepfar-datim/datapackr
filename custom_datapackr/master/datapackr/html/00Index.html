datapackr Data Pack on R datapackr: a grammar of Data Pack manipulation datapackr is the engine behind PEPFAR Data Packs, a tool used annually to aid PEPFAR Country Teams in setting targets for their annual Country Operating Plan (COP). It has three main goals: • Generate Data Packs, either at District or Site Level as directed. • Process and validate Data Packs submitted for review • Prepare Data Pack data for import into DATIM, the PEPFAR data collection database.
